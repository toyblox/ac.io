THE COWSHARE

html/css | squarespace | mailchimp | photoshop


This was my first paid, freelance design + UX/UI project commissioned by a startup called The Cowshare. I taught myself the developer side of SquareSpace in order to manipulate & customize a template they had chosen. The project also included designing a few custom email templates using MailChimp (shown below) with a custom logo, which helped me learn the developer side of the service. The last part of the project was a Facebook cover photo which used a similar custom logo.


You can visit The Cowshare to see the site.


The project included 4 email templates.



Welcome email - when users sign up through their website.



Order Confirmation email - when users submit an order through their website.



A monthly newsletter option with the custom logo.



Another monthly newsletter option with a second custom logo.



Modified custom logos for the Facebook cover photos.
