COOP

html/css | bootstrap | javascript | angularjs | nodeJs | express | google maps api


This was my very first full stack application. I have friends in the city who own chicken coops & always have extra eggs. COOP lets people find and connect with backyard chicken coops in their area so they can get fresh, local eggs. People with coops can also list & manage their coops. I love anything that encourages people (who are already neighbors!), to connect & build a community where everyone can source + benefit.




Landing Page - where users can sign in / sign up



Sign in / Sign up page



Users can also list their coop & fill out details



Once signed in or listed, the user can see a map with all available coops in their area / the coop just listed.
